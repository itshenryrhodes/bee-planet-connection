Beekeeping in ancient civilisations

Beekeeping has roots stretching back thousands of years, intertwined with agriculture, religion, and medicine.

Egypt

  • Hieroglyphs show clay hives and honey harvests.
  • Honey offered to gods and used in embalming.

Greece & Rome

  • Writings of Aristotle and Virgil describe bee behaviour.
  • Honey prized as a sweetener before cane sugar.

Mesopotamia

  • Texts record honey’s medicinal and ritual use.
  • Bees associated with fertility and prosperity.
