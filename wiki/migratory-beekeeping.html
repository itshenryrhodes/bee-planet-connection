Migratory beekeeping

Beekeepers often move colonies to follow nectar flows or supply pollination services. This practice maximises income but adds stress and disease risks.

Benefits

  • Access to multiple nectar flows → more honey yields.
  • Pollination income from high-value crops.
  • Helps avoid forage dearths in one region.

Challenges

  • Transport stress and overheating (heat management critical).
  • Disease spread in high-density pollination zones.
