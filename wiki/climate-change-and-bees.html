Climate change & bees

Bees are vulnerable to changing weather patterns, altered bloom times and increased extreme events.

Impacts

  • Mismatches between flower bloom and bee foraging cycles.
  • Heatwaves causing colony stress.
  • Range shifts in wild bee species.

Adaptation strategies

  • Planting diverse forage (bee-friendly planting).
  • Providing shade, water and insulation.
  • Breeding for resilience (genetics).
