Bee venom & apitherapy

Bee venom (apitoxin) has been used for centuries in folk medicine and is today explored in modern therapies.

Composition

  • Melittin (anti-inflammatory properties).
  • Apamin (affects nervous system).
  • Enzymes such as phospholipase A2.

Uses

  • Arthritis and joint pain (research ongoing).
  • Skin care products.
  • Desensitisation therapy for allergies.

Cautions

  • Risk of severe allergic reaction.
  • Clinical evidence is mixed; more trials needed.
