Hive products in cosmetics

Bee-derived substances are prized for their natural qualities in skincare and beauty products.

Common uses

  • Honey: Moisturiser and antibacterial agent.
  • Beeswax: Emulsifier and protective barrier in creams and balms.
  • Propolis: Used in acne and wound-healing formulations.
  • Royal jelly: Marketed as anti-aging ingredient.

Market trends

Global demand for “clean beauty” drives interest in sustainable apiculture sourcing.
