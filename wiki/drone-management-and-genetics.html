Drone Management & Genetics

Drones contribute to genetic diversity and queen mating quality. Healthy drone populations improve colony performance in the next generation.

Considerations

  • Allow some drone comb during breeding periods.
  • Position mating yards near quality drone producers.
  • Balance with varroa control if using drone brood removal.
