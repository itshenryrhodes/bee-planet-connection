Honey Harvest & Extraction: Basics

Harvest only capped frames (or use a refractometer) to avoid fermentation. Maintain cleanliness, warm room temperatures, and food safety standards.

Steps

  • Uncap with knife or fork; spin gently to avoid comb blowout.
  • Filter coarse wax; allow honey to settle before jarring.
  • Label with origin and batch; store cool and dry.
