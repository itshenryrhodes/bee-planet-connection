Global honey production statistics

Honey is produced on every continent except Antarctica, with large variations in scale and yield.

Top producers

  • China (largest exporter).
  • Turkey, Argentina, India, Ukraine.
  • Mexico and USA as major contributors.

Trends

  • Steady global output, but per-colony yields vary.
  • Price pressures linked to adulteration issues.
  • Growing demand for organic and single-origin honeys.
