Propolis: Harvest & Applications

Propolis is a resinous sealant bees use for hygiene. Harvest via propolis screens and chill to remove. Clean before use.

Uses

  • Tinctures, salves, and varnishes (follow local regulations).
  • Hive disinfectant properties; gap sealing.
