Integrated Pest Management for Bees

IPM blends monitoring, cultural practices, genetics, and targeted treatments to keep colonies healthy with minimal chemical reliance.

Core elements

  • Regular monitoring and thresholds.
  • Hygienic queens and comb renewal.
  • Timed treatments and rotation of actives.
