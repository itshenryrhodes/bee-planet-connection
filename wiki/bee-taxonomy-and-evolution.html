Bee taxonomy & evolution

Bees belong to the superfamily Apoidea. They evolved from wasp ancestors that shifted from hunting to pollen feeding.

Classification

  • ~20,000 bee species globally.
  • Apis: Honey bees (~10 species).
  • Bombus: Bumblebees (~250 species).
  • Many solitary families: Andrenidae, Megachilidae, Halictidae.

Evolution

Fossil bees date back ~100 million years. Co-evolution with flowering plants shaped bee diversity and pollination ecology.
