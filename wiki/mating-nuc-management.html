Mating Nuc Management

Mating nucs need careful feeding and weather protection. Evaluate queens for laying pattern after 2–3 weeks and cull poor performers.

Tips

  • Prevent drifting with distinct landmarks.
  • Keep queens marked; note hatch and mating dates.
  • Introduce to production colonies via cage for acceptance.
