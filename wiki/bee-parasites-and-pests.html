Bee parasites & pests

Beyond diseases, honey bee colonies face threats from parasites and hive invaders.

Examples

  • Varroa destructor: Parasitic mite weakening brood and spreading viruses.
  • Small hive beetle: Larvae damage combs and ferment honey.
  • Wax moths: Destroy stored comb, especially in weak colonies.
  • Tropilaelaps mites: Affect brood in some Asian regions.
