Beekeeping & climate change

Changing climate affects bee forage, pest pressure, and colony stress. Beekeepers adapt with management and advocacy.

Impacts

  • Shifts in flowering times disrupt forage availability.
  • Increased heatwaves demand cooling strategies.
  • New pests and pathogens expand range.

Responses

  • Breeding for resilience (see genetics).
  • Planting climate-adapted forage mixes.
  • Advocacy for pollinator-friendly policy.
