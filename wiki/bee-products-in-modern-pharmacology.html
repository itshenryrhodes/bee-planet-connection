Bee products in modern pharmacology

Bee products are being studied for therapeutic potential in contemporary medicine.

Areas of interest

  • Honey: Antibacterial properties, especially Manuka honey.
  • Propolis: Investigated for antiviral and anticancer compounds.
  • Royal jelly: Possible neuroprotective effects.
  • Venom peptides: Studied for autoimmune and neurological disorders.
