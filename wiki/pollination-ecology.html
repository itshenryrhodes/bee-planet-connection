Pollination ecology

Pollination ecology studies how bees and other pollinators interact with flowering plants in ecosystems.

Key concepts

  • Mutualism between plants and pollinators.
  • Network stability and redundancy.
  • Impact of species loss on food webs.

Relevance to agriculture

Understanding these systems supports sustainable crop production and biodiversity conservation.
