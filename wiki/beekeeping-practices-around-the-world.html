Beekeeping practices around the world

Methods of keeping bees vary with culture, climate, and resources.

Traditional methods

  • Log hives in Africa and Eastern Europe.
  • Clay pot hives in the Mediterranean.
  • Bamboo and woven hives in Asia.

Modern practices

  • Langstroth hives dominate commercial beekeeping globally.
  • Top-bar and Warré hives emphasise low-intervention methods.
