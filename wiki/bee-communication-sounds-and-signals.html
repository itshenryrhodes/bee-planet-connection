Bee communication: sounds & signals

Beyond dances, bees use vibrations and sounds to share information and regulate the colony.

Types of signals

  • Piping: Queens signal before emergence or swarming.
  • Hissing: Worker alarm response when disturbed.
  • Tremble dance: Motivates foragers to unload nectar.
  • Vibration signals: Regulate activity and brood care.

Research

Acoustic monitoring is being integrated into smart hive systems for real-time analysis.
