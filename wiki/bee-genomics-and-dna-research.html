Bee genomics & DNA research

The honey bee genome was sequenced in 2006, unlocking new avenues of research.

Applications

  • Identifying genes linked to hygienic behaviour.
  • Understanding immunity and pathogen resistance.
  • Tracing evolutionary history and subspecies relationships.

Future directions

CRISPR and molecular tools may allow precision breeding and improved disease control.
