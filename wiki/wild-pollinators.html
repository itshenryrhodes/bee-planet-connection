Wild pollinators

Honey bees are crucial, but wild pollinators like bumblebees, solitary bees, flies, butterflies and beetles provide essential services too.

Roles

  • Pollinate crops poorly served by honey bees (tomatoes, blueberries).
  • Ensure resilience through biodiversity.
  • Support wild plant reproduction.
