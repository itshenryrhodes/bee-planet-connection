Artificial Swarms & Bailey Change

Artificial swarms redirect swarming impulse while keeping bees. Bailey comb change replaces old brood comb gradually.

Notes

  • Mark the queen beforehand to simplify manipulation.
  • Feed if nectar scarce; avoid stressing colonies during poor weather.
