Robbing prevention & hive security

Recognise robbing

  • Frantic flight, fighting at the entrance, wax crumbs on landing board.

Immediate actions

  • Reduce entrances; install robbing screens.
  • Stop open feeding; remove exposed honey/comb.

Ongoing security

  • Strap hives; use lockable stands; mark equipment.
  • Control wasps; avoid drips during extraction.
