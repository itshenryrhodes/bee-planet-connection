Honey adulteration & testing

Honey fraud undermines markets and pollinator-supporting livelihoods. Adulteration includes sugar syrups, mislabelling and filtration.

Common adulteration

  • Sugar syrups blended in.
  • Ultra-filtration removing pollen to mask origin.
  • Mislabelled floral or geographic source.

Testing methods

  • NMR profiling and isotope analysis.
  • Pollen analysis for origin verification.
  • Mass spectrometry for adulterant detection.
