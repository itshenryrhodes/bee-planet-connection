Bee memory & learning

Bees show sophisticated learning abilities that help them thrive in complex environments.

Types of learning

  • Associative learning of colours and scents.
  • Spatial memory for flower patches.
  • Ability to generalise patterns and shapes.

Research

Studies on bee cognition inform neuroscience and AI, offering models of efficient learning systems.
