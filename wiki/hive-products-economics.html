Hive products & economics

Beekeeping supports income through honey, wax, pollen, propolis and pollination. Economics vary by region and scale.

Revenue streams

  • Honey: Main product worldwide; influenced by type and branding.
  • Wax: Value-add via candles, cosmetics (rendering).
  • Pollen & propolis: Supplements and health products.
  • Pollination contracts: Major revenue in monoculture systems.

Costs

  • Equipment and hives.
  • Feed and treatments for Varroa.
  • Labour and transport.
