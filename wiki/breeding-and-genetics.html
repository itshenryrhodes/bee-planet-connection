Breeding & genetics

Selective breeding strengthens honey bee resilience. Balancing productivity with traits like gentleness and disease resistance is key.

Traits targeted

  • Hygienic behaviour against brood disease.
  • Gentle temperament for urban beekeeping.
  • High honey yield and low swarming tendency.

Methods

  • Open mating in drone-saturated areas.
  • Instrumental insemination for controlled lines.
  • Selection across seasons to test durability.

Genetic diversity

Diversity reduces inbreeding issues and improves resilience to pests like Varroa.
