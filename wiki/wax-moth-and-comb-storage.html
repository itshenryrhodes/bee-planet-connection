Wax moth & comb storage

  • Freeze frames (−18°C, 24–48h) before storage to kill eggs/larvae.
  • Store dry with airflow and light; stack with spacers.
  • Consider acetic acid fumigation for sterilising comb (follow safety guidance).
  • Rotate out old, dark comb (biosecurity).
