Urban beekeeping

City beekeeping has grown popular. Rooftops and gardens host colonies, offering forage diversity but raising questions about welfare and sustainability.

Benefits

  • Rich urban forage (parks, gardens, roadside plantings).
  • Community engagement and education.
  • Local honey with unique floral signatures.

Challenges

  • High hive density may stress forage (forage ecology).
  • Neighbours’ concerns about stings.
  • Hive placement and building access issues.
