Africanised Honey Bees: Overview

Africanised bees can be more defensive and swarm-prone. In affected regions, management emphasises requeening with gentle stock and careful apiary placement.

Key points

  • Maintain marked, known queens; replace feral takeovers.
  • Site hives away from footpaths and livestock.
  • Use proper PPE and defensive handling protocols.
