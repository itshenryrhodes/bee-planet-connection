Honey Labelling & Compliance: Basics

Labels must meet your jurisdiction’s food law. Include identity of the food, net quantity, origin, and contact details. Keep batch records for traceability.

  • Use accurate net weights and legible typography.
  • Avoid medicinal claims; follow trading standards guidance.

Note: Check your local regulations for exact wording and font size rules.
