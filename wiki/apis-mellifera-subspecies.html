Apis mellifera subspecies

The western honey bee (Apis mellifera) has many subspecies adapted to local climates and management preferences.

Examples

  • Italian (A. m. ligustica): Gentle, productive, widely used.
  • Carniolan (A. m. carnica): Calm, fast spring build-up.
  • Buckfast: Hybrid line bred by Brother Adam for productivity.
  • Africanised bees: Hybrid descendants in the Americas; defensive reputation.
