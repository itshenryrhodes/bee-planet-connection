Bee breeding for traits

Selective breeding enhances desirable qualities in honey bee stocks.

Common traits

  • Gentle temperament for urban beekeeping.
  • High honey yield and brood viability.
  • Resistance to Varroa mites and diseases.
  • Winter hardiness and spring build-up.

Methods

  • Instrumental insemination.
  • Isolated mating stations.
  • Marker-assisted selection (emerging).
