Pollinator conservation programs

Many regions have launched initiatives to address pollinator declines.

Examples

  • EU Pollinators Initiative: Protect habitats and limit pesticide risks.
  • US Pollinator Partnership: Awareness, research and habitat creation.
  • UK National Pollinator Strategy: Landscape-scale conservation actions.

Role of beekeepers

Beekeepers contribute by sharing monitoring data and promoting bee-friendly planting.
