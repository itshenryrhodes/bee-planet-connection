Bees in agriculture

Managed and wild bees are essential for food security, improving both crop yields and quality.

Major crops

  • Almonds, apples, blueberries, cucumbers.
  • Oilseed rape and sunflower.
  • Coffee and cacao in the tropics.

Challenges

  • Reliance on migratory beekeeping.
  • Pesticide exposure in intensive systems.
  • Competition with wild pollinators.
