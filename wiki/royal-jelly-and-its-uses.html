Royal jelly & its uses

Royal jelly is a secretion fed to developing larvae and the queen, valued by humans for health and cosmetic applications.

Composition

  • Rich in proteins, lipids, vitamins, and unique fatty acids.
  • Contains royalactin, linked to queen development.

Uses

  • Marketed as dietary supplement.
  • Ingredient in skin creams and tonics.
  • Research on immunomodulatory and antioxidant properties continues.
