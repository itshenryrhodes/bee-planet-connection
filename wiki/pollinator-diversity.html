Pollinator diversity

Honey bees are just one part of the pollinator picture. Wild species sustain ecosystems and provide redundancy in crop systems.

Groups

  • Bumblebees: Efficient in cooler climates, buzz pollination specialists.
  • Solitary bees: Mason, leafcutter, mining bees with diverse nesting habits.
  • Hoverflies: Double as pollinators and aphid predators.

Conservation

Habitat diversity and reduced pesticide use benefit wild pollinators alongside forage ecology.
