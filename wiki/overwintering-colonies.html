Overwintering colonies

  • Nutrition: Ensure adequate stores; consider fondant if light.
  • Mite control: Post-harvest Varroa treatment; monitor.
  • Insulation & moisture: See insulation & ventilation.
  • Entrance & pests: Reduce drafts; deter mice; secure roofs.
  • Spring build-up: Early checks, add space before congestion.
