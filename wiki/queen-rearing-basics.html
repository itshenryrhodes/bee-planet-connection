Queen Rearing: Basics

Raising your own queens allows selection for temperament, productivity, and disease resistance. Start small with simple grafting or cell punching techniques.

Steps

  • Strong starter colony; consistent feeding.
  • Transfer young larvae to cups; protect from chilling.
  • Finish cells, then distribute to mating nucs.
