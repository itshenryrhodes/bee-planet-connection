Honey types & characteristics

Honey varies in flavour, colour and aroma depending on floral source, region and season.

Categories

  • Monofloral: Derived mainly from one source (e.g. acacia, heather, manuka).
  • Multifloral/wildflower: Mixed sources; flavour shifts seasonally.
  • Honeydew: From plant-sucking insects; darker, mineral-rich.

Quality factors

  • Moisture content: affects fermentation risk.
  • HMF (hydroxymethylfurfural) rises with heating/storage.
  • Authenticity testing combats adulteration.
