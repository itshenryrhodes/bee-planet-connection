Swarm Prevention Through Space Management

Congestion triggers swarming. Timely supers, comb renewal, and brood nest management keep colonies productive.

  • Add supers ahead of flows; avoid sudden crowding.
  • Replace dark comb; ensure queen has fresh laying space.
  • Split strong colonies if queen cells appear.
