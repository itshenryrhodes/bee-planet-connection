Varroa Treatment Options

Combine monitoring with timely treatment to keep mite loads below damaging levels. Rotate actives to avoid resistance.

Options

  • Oxalic acid: Trickled or vaporised during low brood.
  • Formic acid: Penetrates capped cells; temperature sensitive.
  • Thymol blends: Slow release in warm weather.
  • Synthetics: Follow label; watch resistance.
