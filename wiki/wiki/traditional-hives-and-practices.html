Traditional hives & practices

Before modern framed hives, beekeepers used skeps, hollow logs, clay pots and basket hives. Many are still used in low-input beekeeping and cultural traditions.

Examples

  • Skeps: Straw baskets; outlawed in some countries due to disease control issues.
  • Log hives: Carved trunks; promote natural comb building.
  • Clay hives: Used in Mediterranean and Middle Eastern traditions.

Modern relevance

Traditional methods inform bee welfare debates and natural beekeeping approaches.
