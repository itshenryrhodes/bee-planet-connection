Beekeeping tools & protective gear

Practical beekeeping relies on basic tools and safety equipment to manage hives effectively and calmly.

Essential tools

  • Hive tool: For prying frames and scraping propolis.
  • Smoker: Calms bees by masking alarm pheromones.
  • Bee brush: Gently clears bees from comb.

Protective gear

  • Suits and veils: Full or jacket style; light colours preferred.
  • Gloves: Leather or nitrile; balance dexterity with protection.
  • Footwear: Sturdy boots reduce sting risk at ankles.
