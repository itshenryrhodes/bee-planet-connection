Urban Apiary Siting Guidelines

Good siting reduces conflict and supports bee health. Consider flight paths, water, and discretion.

Checklist

  • Screening to lift flight lines above head height.
  • Secure stands, shade at midday, nearby water source.
  • Communicate proactively with neighbours/landlords.
