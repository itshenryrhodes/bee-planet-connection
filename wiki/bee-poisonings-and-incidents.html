Bee poisonings & incidents

Bee kill events can result from pesticide misuse, contaminated water sources, or accidental exposure.

Signs

  • Large numbers of dead or twitching bees near hives.
  • Bees with extended proboscises or trembling.
  • Sudden forager losses without brood decline.

Actions

  • Report to authorities or beekeeper associations.
  • Collect samples for lab testing.
  • Engage with farmers on spraying schedules (see pesticides).
