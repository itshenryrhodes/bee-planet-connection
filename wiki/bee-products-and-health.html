Bee products & human health

Bee products are widely used in traditional remedies and modern health products, though evidence varies.

Examples

  • Honey: Antibacterial and wound-healing properties.
  • Propolis: Antimicrobial, used in tinctures and lozenges.
  • Pollen: Supplements, though may trigger allergies.
  • Royal jelly: Promoted for vitality, evidence mixed.
  • Venom therapy: Experimental use for arthritis and MS.
