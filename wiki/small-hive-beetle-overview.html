Small Hive Beetle: Overview

Small Hive Beetle (SHB) stresses colonies by sliming combs and fouling stores. Risk varies by region; follow national guidance.

Identification

  • Dark beetles, rapid movement; larvae with spines and three pairs of legs.
  • Signs: fermented odour, slimed comb, bees avoiding frames.

Management

  • Maintain strong colonies; avoid leaving supers/frames exposed.
  • Use traps and good apiary hygiene.
  • Report suspected cases per local regulations.
