Bee culture & tradition

Bees appear in myth, religion and art worldwide. Honey symbolises purity, fertility and abundance.

Examples

  • Egypt: Bees linked to royalty and sun god Ra.
  • Greece: Honey offered to gods; bees connected to Artemis.
  • Christianity: Bees as models of diligence and order.
  • Modern culture: Bees as climate and biodiversity icons.
