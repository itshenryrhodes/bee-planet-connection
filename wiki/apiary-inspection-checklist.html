Apiary Inspection Checklist

  • Queen status: eggs/larvae, brood pattern.
  • Stores: honey and pollen frames.
  • Space: brood nest room; super requirements.
  • Swarm signs: queen cells, congestion.
  • Pest/disease: varroa counts, brood health, chalkbrood/AFB signs.
  • Hardware: frames straight, comb age, hive integrity.
