Drone brood removal for Varroa control

Varroa prefer drone brood. Offering frames of drone foundation then removing/ freezing capped drone brood reduces mite loads.

  • Insert drone frame at brood edge; inspect weekly in build-up.
  • Cut out capped drone brood before emergence; discard or freeze.
  • Combine with IPM (monitoring + treatments).
