Wax rendering & uses

Beeswax is a valuable byproduct. Rendering cleans it for reuse in foundation, crafts and cosmetics.

Rendering methods

  • Solar melter: Simple, free energy, but weather dependent.
  • Water bath: Wax melts into hot water, debris sinks.
  • Steam extraction: Efficient for cappings and old comb.

Uses

  • Candles and polish.
  • Cosmetics, salves and balms.
  • Foundation sheets for frames.
