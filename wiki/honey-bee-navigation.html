Honey bee navigation

Honey bees are remarkable navigators, combining multiple sensory cues to forage and return home.

Mechanisms

  • Sun compass: Use of solar position and polarised light.
  • Landmarks: Trees, hedgerows, buildings.
  • Magnetoreception: Possible use of Earth’s magnetic field.
  • Odour plumes: Scents guide bees to flowers and hive entrances.

Communication

Navigation is linked to the waggle dance, encoding distance and direction.
