Nutrition and pollen diversity

Pollen is the primary protein source for bees, and its diversity is critical for colony resilience.

Why diversity matters

  • Different plants provide varying amino acids and micronutrients.
  • Monocultures can lead to deficiencies and weakened immune systems.
  • Diverse pollen improves brood development and disease resistance.

Implications

  • Encourages planting bee-friendly gardens with multiple flowering species.
  • Guides beekeepers in supplementing diets during dearth periods.
