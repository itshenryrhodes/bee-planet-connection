Drone biology and roles

Drones are the male caste in honey bee colonies, specialised for reproduction.

Characteristics

  • Larger eyes for spotting queens in flight.
  • No stingers; do not forage or build comb.
  • Fed by worker bees.

Function

  • Main role is mating with virgin queens during nuptial flights.
  • Transmit genetic diversity across colonies.

Colony dynamics

  • Produced in spring and summer.
  • Often expelled before winter to conserve resources.
