Small hive beetle & Tropilaelaps

Two invasive pests cause global concern for apiculture: the small hive beetle (Aethina tumida) and Tropilaelaps mites. Both spread quickly and damage colonies severely.

Small hive beetle (SHB)

  • Native to Africa; spread to North America, Australia, southern Europe.
  • Larvae tunnel through comb, ruin honey stores, cause slime and fermentation.
  • Adults hide in crevices; difficult to spot in early stages.
