Honey in culture & religion

Honey has long been valued not just as food but as a sacred substance in many cultures.

Examples

  • Hinduism: Honey offered in rituals (Madhu-abhisheka).
  • Judaism: Apples and honey at Rosh Hashanah symbolise a sweet new year.
  • Islam: Qur’an references honey as healing.
  • Ancient Greece: Honey offered to gods and the dead.
