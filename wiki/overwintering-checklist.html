Overwintering Checklist

  • Healthy, young queen with solid brood pattern by late summer.
  • Adequate stores (region dependent; often 18–25 kg honey equivalent).
  • Effective mite management complete before autumn brood ends.
  • Top insulation, controlled ventilation, reduced entrances.
  • Mouse guards fitted; hives strapped and raised from ground.
  • Late-season feeding finished in time to ripen stores.
