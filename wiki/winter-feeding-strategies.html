Winter Feeding Strategies

Feeding supports colonies short on stores. Choose syrup in autumn to build reserves; use fondant or dry sugar in mid-winter emergencies.

Guidance

  • Autumn: 2:1 syrup after harvest; finish feeding before cold snaps.
  • Mid-winter: Fondant directly above cluster with insulation.
  • Spring: Light 1:1 syrup to stimulate if necessary (avoid robbing).
