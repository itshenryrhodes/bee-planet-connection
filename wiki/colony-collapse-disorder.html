Colony collapse disorder

CCD emerged in the mid-2000s, marked by sudden colony losses with absent adult bees but brood and food left behind.

Symptoms

  • Empty hives with queen, brood and stores intact.
  • Few or no dead bees near the hive.

Suspected causes

  • Viruses and Varroa as key stressors.
  • Pesticide exposure (see pesticides).
  • Poor nutrition and forage diversity.
  • Synergistic stress: climate, transport, pathogens together.
