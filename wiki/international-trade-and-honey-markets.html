International trade & honey markets

Honey is traded globally, but authenticity scandals and price pressures challenge producers.

Trends

  • China is the largest exporter; EU and US major importers.
  • Adulteration scandals drive stricter testing (see blog).
  • Premium markets for monofloral honeys like manuka.

Challenges

  • Price competition undermines small-scale beekeepers.
  • Traceability and certification increasingly demanded.
