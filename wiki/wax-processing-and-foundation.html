Wax Processing & Foundation

Beeswax is valuable. Process cappings and old comb into clean blocks for candles, cosmetics, or foundation sheets.

Tips

  • Use a double boiler or solar melter; avoid overheating.
  • Strain through muslin; allow to cool slowly for clarity.
  • Consider foundationless frames for natural comb where appropriate.
