Hive microbiome

Bee colonies host diverse microbial communities that influence nutrition, immunity and disease resistance.

Main microbes

  • Gut bacteria: Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium species aid digestion.
  • Fungi: Yeasts help in pollen fermentation.
  • Pathogens: Nosema spp. can disrupt balance.

Research & applications

  • Probiotic supplements in beekeeping.
  • Microbiome as indicator of colony health.
