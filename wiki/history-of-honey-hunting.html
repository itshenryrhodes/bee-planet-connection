History of honey hunting

Humans have sought honey for tens of thousands of years. Cave art from Spain (~8,000 years old) shows figures collecting honey from wild hives.

Traditions

  • Asia: Honey hunting of giant Apis dorsata in Nepal remains a cultural practice.
  • Africa: Use of honeyguides (birds) to locate wild colonies.
  • Europe: Medieval forest beekeeping traditions in logs and trees.
