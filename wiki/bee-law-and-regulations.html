Bee law & regulations

Beekeeping is subject to specific laws to protect both bees and consumers.

Areas of regulation

  • Compulsory hive registration in many countries.
  • Notifiable diseases (e.g. American foulbrood).
  • Honey standards and labelling rules.
  • Movement of colonies across borders.

International frameworks

  • Codex Alimentarius honey standard.
  • OIE (World Organisation for Animal Health) disease reporting.
