Record Keeping for Beekeepers

Consistent records allow comparisons across seasons and colonies. Basic notes are better than none; digital or paper both work.

What to log

  • Date, weather, forage conditions.
  • Queen status, temperament, brood pattern.
  • Varroa counts, treatments, outcomes.
  • Swarm cells, splits, requeening actions.
  • Harvest weights and dates.
