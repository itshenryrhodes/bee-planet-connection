Chalkbrood: Diagnosis & Management

Chalkbrood is a fungal disease affecting larvae in cool, damp conditions. Often self-limiting in strong colonies.

What to look for

  • White or grey “mummies” in cells or on the floor.
  • Patchy brood and reduced population growth.

Management

  • Improve ventilation and insulation; replace old comb.
  • Requeen if persistent; select hygienic stock.
  • Avoid chilling brood during inspections.
