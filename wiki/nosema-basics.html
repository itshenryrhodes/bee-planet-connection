Nosema: Basics

Nosema is a microsporidian infection linked to dysentery-like signs and reduced colony performance. Good hygiene and strong nutrition help resilience.

Signs

  • Staining near entrances in cooler months.
  • Reduced foraging strength and longevity.

Management

  • Replace old comb; reduce stress; ensure adequate forage.
  • Follow local veterinary guidance before using treatments.
