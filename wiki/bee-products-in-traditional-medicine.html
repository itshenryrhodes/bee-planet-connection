Bee products in traditional medicine

For millennia, cultures worldwide have valued bee products for healing and health.

Examples

  • Honey: Wound dressing in Egypt and Ayurveda.
  • Propolis: Antiseptic in Eastern Europe and Asia.
  • Beeswax: Salves and ointments in Mediterranean traditions.
  • Venom: Used in Chinese medicine for arthritis relief.
