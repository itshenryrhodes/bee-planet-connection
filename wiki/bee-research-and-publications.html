Bee research & publications

Research drives modern apiculture. Journals and organisations publish studies on bee health, forage and management.

Key organisations

  • Apimondia: International federation hosting congresses.
  • IBRA: Publishes the Journal of Apicultural Research.
  • USDA Bee Research Labs: Studies pests and pollination.
