Pesticides & regulation

Pesticides play a major role in agriculture but present risks for pollinators. Policy evolves as science improves.

Key pesticide groups

  • Neonicotinoids: Widely restricted in EU/UK; evidence of sublethal effects.
  • Fungicides: Often overlooked, but can synergise with insecticides.
  • Herbicides: Indirectly harm bees by reducing forage plants.

Regulatory frameworks

  • EU precautionary principle bans on neonicotinoids.
  • Integrated Pest Management (IPM) promoted globally.
  • Pollinator risk assessment protocols updated by EFSA/US EPA.
