Making & managing nucleus colonies (nucs)

Nucs provide spare queens, swarm control options and winter insurance.

How to make a nuc

  • From a strong colony: 2–3 frames brood + adhering bees, 1–2 food frames.
  • Add a mated queen or a ripe queen cell from queen rearing.

Management

  • Feed lightly until foragers develop; avoid robbing (see robbing).
  • Scale up to full hives or keep as resource colonies.
