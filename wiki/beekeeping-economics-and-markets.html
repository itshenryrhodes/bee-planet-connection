Beekeeping economics & markets

Beekeeping is not only a craft but also an economic activity linked to global trade.

Inputs

  • Hive equipment and maintenance.
  • Supplementary feed and treatments.
  • Labour costs for commercial apiaries.

Markets

  • Honey as primary revenue stream.
  • Secondary products: wax, pollen, propolis.
  • Pollination services as growing business.
