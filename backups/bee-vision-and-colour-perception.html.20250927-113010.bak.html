Bee vision & colour perception

Honey bees perceive the world differently from humans, with unique adaptations for foraging.

Spectrum sensitivity

  • See ultraviolet light invisible to humans.
  • Do not see red; perceive it as black.
  • Strongly sensitive to blue and green.

Foraging benefits

UV patterns on petals (“nectar guides”) help bees locate rewards efficiently.
