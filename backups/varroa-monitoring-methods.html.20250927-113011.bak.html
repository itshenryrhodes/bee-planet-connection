Varroa Monitoring Methods

Monitoring guides treatment timing and evaluates effectiveness. Choose a method that suits your practice and local guidance.

Methods

  • Alcohol wash: Most accurate; ~300 bees; count mites.
  • Sugar roll: Non-lethal; good estimate if done carefully.
  • Natural drop: Sticky board counts over 3 days; less precise.
