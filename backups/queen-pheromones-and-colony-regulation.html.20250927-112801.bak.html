Queen pheromones & colony regulation

The queen maintains colony cohesion largely through pheromones.

Key functions

  • Inhibit worker ovary development.
  • Signal queen health and presence.
  • Influence swarming and supersedure decisions.

Implications

Understanding pheromones helps in queen rearing and colony management.
