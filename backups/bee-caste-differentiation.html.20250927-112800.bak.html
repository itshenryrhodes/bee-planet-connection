Bee caste differentiation

Despite genetic similarity, honey bee larvae develop into distinct castes depending on diet and environment.

Factors

  • Royal jelly stimulates queen development.
  • Pollen and brood food guide worker development.
  • Drone cells are larger and fed differently.

Epigenetics

Research highlights DNA methylation and nutrition as key mechanisms of caste determination.
