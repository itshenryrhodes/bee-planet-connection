Our mission

To make credible apiculture knowledge easy to find, easy to understand, and easy to act on — anywhere in the world.

Bee Planet Connection is a community project, curated and maintained by volunteers. We publish guides, host a wiki, and maintain a global directory for the beekeeping and pollinator community.
