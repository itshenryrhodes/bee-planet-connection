Our mission

To make credible apiculture knowledge easy to find, easy to understand, and easy to act on — anywhere in the world.

What we publish

  • Practical guides and seasonal how-tos for all levels.
  • A growing wiki of bee biology, husbandry and habitat.
  • A global directory of organisations, journals and NGOs.
  • Blog posts covering research highlights and current affairs.

How we work

  • Open, vendor-neutral, science-minded.
  • Clear editorial standards (citations, dated updates).
  • Accessibility and performance as first-class citizens.
  • Privacy-friendly analytics; no ads, no tracking profiles.

Get involved

Have an article idea, correction or dataset to share? We’d love to hear from you.
