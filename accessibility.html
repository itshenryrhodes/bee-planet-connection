Accessibility statement

We want as many people as possible to be able to use Bee Planet Connection.

Our commitment

We are working to ensure this website is accessible to everyone by following the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, aiming for level AA compliance.

Measures we’ve taken

  • Semantic HTML with clear landmarks (<header>, <main>, <footer>).
  • Accessible, keyboard-operable navigation (including a “skip to content” link).
  • Responsive layouts and readable typography.
  • High-contrast colour palette with a dark-mode option.
  • Descriptive link text and button labels where possible.
  • Images intended as content support alt attributes.

Compatibility and technology

This site is designed to work with the latest versions of major browsers, including Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge. It should work on modern mobile devices and desktop operating systems.

Known limitations

We’re aware of some areas that may not fully meet WCAG 2.1 AA yet:

  • Some older images may be missing descriptive alternative text.
  • Third-party embeds or links may not fully meet our standards.

We are actively improving these areas as we build out content.

Feedback and contact

If you encounter accessibility barriers or have suggestions, please let us know. We’ll do our best to fix issues promptly.

Send accessibility feedback

Requesting content in alternative formats

Need information in a different format (e.g., large print, easy read, audio)? Contact us and tell us which page you need and the format required.

Enforcement

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may be able to escalate your complaint through relevant national bodies or digital accessibility organisations.
