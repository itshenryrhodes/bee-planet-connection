Integrated Pest Management for Bees

IPM is an attitude as much as a toolkit: monitor routinely, prevent where you can, and treat only as needed with a plan for resistance.

Core pillars

  • Monitoring: regular mite checks and disease observation. See Varroa Monitoring Methods.
  • Prevention: spacing, genetics, brood breaks, hygiene.
  • Intervention: rotate modes of action; time for best efficacy.

Building a season plan

Map forage flow, swarm control, and treatment windows. Align brood breaks with treatments (e.g., OAV during caging). See Treatment Options.

Records that matter

Track mite counts, treatments used, and outcomes to guide next year. See Record Keeping.
