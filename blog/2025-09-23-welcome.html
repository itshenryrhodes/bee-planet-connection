Welcome — why Bee Planet Connection exists

23 September 2025 #apiculture #project

Thanks for stopping by. Bee Planet Connection is a clean, fast, no-nonsense hub for apiculture — organisations, research, practical methods, and trustworthy links — built so you don’t have to wade through noise to find what matters.

What you’ll find

  • Directory of reputable bodies (Apimondia, IBRA, and more) with clear descriptions.
  • Knowledge pages that surface methods and journals without fluff.
  • Blog updates on what’s new, plus notes from research.

Get involved

If you’d like to suggest a resource or correction, open an issue on the GitHub repo or drop a note via the contact link on the homepage.

Onwards — and save the bees.
