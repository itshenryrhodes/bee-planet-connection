Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and keep things simple.

What we collect

We use Google Analytics 4 in a privacy-friendly way to understand how people use this site. This helps us improve content and usability.

  • Page views (which pages are visited, how often)
  • Anonymous technical details (device type, browser)
  • On-site search terms (if you use the search box)

What we don’t collect

  • No advertising profiles
  • No cross-site tracking
  • No personal identifiers (like name, email, address)

Your choices

You can accept or reject analytics cookies using the banner shown when you first visit. You can also clear your choice any time by clearing your browser’s local storage and cookies.

If you reject, no analytics data will be sent.

Data retention

Analytics data is stored in aggregated form by Google Analytics and retained for a limited period (currently 14 months).

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us.
